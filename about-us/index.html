About-Us

"We are on a mission to do something meaningful and bring a fundamental change in lives of our farmers and customers at large". It gives us great joy when we are able to increase income of our farmers and and bring a smile of the faces of customers as they enjoy the freshness of the produce, because what we eat is the fuel that drives us.
So, we started Magic Farm as a solution to benefit the farmer who is producing organic food products as well as the customers who seek to eat healthy at affordable prices. Magic Farm is committed to being a trustworthy & Innovative leader by providing genuine True wellness products. We work with Thousands of small farmers in India to cultivate ten of thousands of acres of sustainable Organic Farmland.

Our Vision

To be a Vehicle of Consciousness in the global market by creating an holistic, sustainable business modality, which inspires, promotes and supports True Wellness and respect for all Beings and for Mother Nature..

Our Mission

To be a trustworthy and innovative global leader in providing genuine organic True Wellness products and solutions for conscious, healthy living.

Our Values

  1. Service to all.
  2. Total integrity.
  3. Absolute commitment to quality.
  4. Respect and devotion to Mother Nature.
  5. No compromise on being who we are.

Our Commitment

  1. To deliver genuine organic foods and products to our consumers.
  2. To introduce a unique and successful business model that is committed to service and integrity, and benefits all.
  3. To support natural, sustainable, organic, agriculture practices that serve and protect Mother Nature.
  4. To support the livelihood and well-being of farmers and tribal wildcrafters across rural India.
